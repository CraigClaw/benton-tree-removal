# Benton Tree Removal Pros Lead Buyer Prospect Plan

## Opportunity

Tree removal has urgent storm demand, good ticket size, and clear quote criteria by location, hazard, and access.

## Search Queries Reviewed

- Benton AR tree removal service
- tree service Benton AR

## Best Buyer Profile

- Local business serving Benton and Saline County.
- Weak website or no city/service page coverage.
- Answers calls quickly.
- Wants tree removal, emergency tree service, stump grinding, tree trimming leads.
- Will accept tracked calls/forms and give feedback on lead quality.

## Outreach Script

> Hi, is this the right person for new tree removal jobs? I built a Benton-focused quote request website for tree removal, emergency tree service, stump grinding, tree trimming. I am looking for one local company to send the leads to first. Would you be open to taking a few tracked leads as a trial and only paying if the quality makes sense?

## Trial Offer

- 7 days or first 5 qualified leads.
- Qualified lead: call over 60 seconds or form with name, phone, city, project type, and details.
- Start with pay-per-lead by job type, then move to exclusive monthly rental after proof.
